Wireframe Design

The UI is the same for all pages, i used a simple yet cute and professional design.

Web Pages and Their Purpose

  • Home: Introduces visitors to my portfolio and provides an overview of what to expect.
  • About: Shares my background, interests, and goals.
  • Contact: Provides a way for visitors to reach out, and a link to all my social media
  • Professional: Highlights my experiences, skills, achievements, also my future goals.
  • Personality: Explores my personality traits and interests.
  • Personal Development: Discusses my journey and reflections on personal growth.

Theme of the Site

The theme of my site is to create a warm, inviting, and professional atmosphere. The color palette will include soft pastels to convey a sense of calm and approachability, complemented by vibrant graphics that reflect my passion for technology and creativity. The target audience includes peers in the academic community.

Features of the Website

    In the future I will implement features such as:

  • Gallery: Showcasing my projects and achievements.
  • Video: Incorporating video presentations of my work or experiences.
  • Audio: Featuring a section for my favorite music or podcasts.
